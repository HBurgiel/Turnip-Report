# Squash, Onion, Butter, and Sage
Based on {Savory Bread Pudding with Roasted Butternut Squash, Onion, and Sage}[http://www.lemonythyme.com/savory-bread-pudding-with-roasted-butternut-squash-onion-sage/] by Libby with Lemony Thyme.

(Sage optional)

- Dice
  - small butternut squash
- Toss with  
  - small, thinly sliced onion
  - 3 cloves chopped garlic
- Stir in
  - 1/2 t salt
  - 1/2 t thyme
  - 1/4 t black pepper
- Microwave 5 minutes on high

- Cube
  - 8 c bread
